Occasions

Secretary day, weddings, birthdays, and even prom. There are countless occasions that we can help you make special.

    Graduation

  • When it comes to graduation and prom, we prefer to start taking in crosage and bouquet orders a month and a half in advance. This will help us provide you with a worry-free and swift service. Please contatct us through email or phone to place orders.

    Weddings

  • Our wedding prices vary depending on the availability of flowers you require, what flowers will be used within you arrangements and also the time of the year that your wedding is for. Come see us in our store for a free consultation.

    Secretary's day

  • Believe it or not, secretary day is one of our most busiest days of the year. It is recommend to pre-order at least 2 weeks in advance so you can beat the rush.
